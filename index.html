How will your money be spent?

My friends and local volunteers are currently in Sunamganj working on the ground
to distribute dry food, hot meals and providing aid
to those affected by the severe flooding

Food

The victims are brought to dry land, fed, clothed and sheltered. The community are working together to source rice, lentils and other food and water. Local volunteers and my friends cook around the clock and hand out food.

Shelter

The community is working together to house the victims temporarily on the mainland. We are working together to charge phones in areas that still have networks so that families can contact their loved ones and gather evidence of the catastrophe.

Future

Your money will help pay for everything in between, fuel for transport, equipment needed to support this operation. Should this campaign be successful, I will use funds to help rebuild the villages destroyed by the floods.

Their lives depend on your support

Families have been separated, stranded, lost loved ones and their animals; they have lost their homes, businesses and hope for any future. 80% of the land is flooded, and 120 million in crops, fisheries, and infrastructure have been destroyed. They have lost mobile network, electricity and water supply as well. The people of Sunamganj need your help.
